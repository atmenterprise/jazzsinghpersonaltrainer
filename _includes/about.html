About Me

Hi, I’m Jaz, an industry qualified Level 3 Personal Trainer & Nutritionist with over 10 years of experience in the health & fitness industry.

For the last 18 years I have been living with a rare auto-immune condition, which resulted in me being wheelchair bound, requiring 25 surgeries, and told I may not have kids. Now I’m a father of three and walking again. I took my health into my own hands to win the battle and now just manage my unpredictable condition with positive lifestyle changes.

This brought me to work in the fitness industry to motivate (while showing empathy) to get the best results for my clients. All have lost weight, become healthier, fitter, stronger & confident.

I recently completed a year of mentoring by 2 leading coaches. I also volunteered as an Expert Patient Programme Tutor in the past, teaching others living with long-term health conditions, how to manage their health by managing their symptoms. This allowed them to use the new lifestyle habits to live a healthier & independent life.
