One to One Personal Training

Working with a personal trainer helps you reach your goal faster while teaching you how to independently sustain your results after the process is complete.



WHO'S IT FOR?

This is great for anyone who would like to follow a personalised programme to reach their health and fitness related goal faster.

It is for someone who is unsure where to begin, have tried many times before and failed, or have stopped seeing any results in what they set out to achieve.

You will benefit from having someone to be accountable to and help you’re training by teaching you the correct techniques for exercises and understand nutrition and how to apply it.


WHAT'S INCLUDED?

  • Receive a personalised nutrition plan while trying to keep your lifestyle in mind all while increasing your nutrition knowledge and promoting healthy eating habits.
  • Nutrition plan adjustments because these will be essential as your progress will slow down the more body fat you drop.
  • Training plan designed relative to your goal and starting place to help you gain more confidence and experience in the gym while boosting activity outside the gym.
  • Training plan adjustments for continued progress by breaking plateaus.
  • Regular tracking and reviews of progress with feedback and actions for weeks ahead.
  • Photos, scales and how you feel will be used to measure progress.

    I will deliver improvements across the board in your weight, body fat, body tone, posture, physical strength and fitness, and performance. You will also feel the difference in measures such as mood, stress, sleep, digestion, confidence, and energy.
