Booking Process

A Simple Two-Step process to get started on your health goals. Fill in few basic health questions about you or your partner and schedule a 30-minutes session with me.

Please remember to use the same email address and first name/last name when submitting both the forms and appointment.


Step 1: Health Questionnaire Form

Fill in the Form* with your health details. Questions include basic questions about your health, your current diet, your problems and the goal you are trying to achieve with me.

Submit your Health Details »

*The data collected in the form is ONLY meant for me i.e. Jaz Singh to review and understand your health problems and targets. Any data submitted in the form will NOT be shared to any other third party applications, adv., health professional or companies. Read more about our Privacy and GDPR policy.

Step 2: Schedule an Appointment

Schedule a complimentary 30-minute appointment with me. This session will include a discussion on your health goals and suggested plan to help you achieve your goal.

Book an Appointment »

[Mandatory] Please complete the health questionnaires (step-1) before continuing with the appointment booking (step-2).
