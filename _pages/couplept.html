2:1 Personal Training

Share all the benefits of one to one personal training.
Fun fact, studies have shown that exercising with a friend or family member can increase calories burned by up to 25%.



WHO'S IT FOR?

If you have a common health and fitness related goal and can workout at the same time, then this is a great way to share the experience of personal training and achieve your goals in a faster time frame.

Whether you both have the same experience in the gym or not, you'll still be individually supported to reach your respective goals as you train together.

You are less likely to quit the process early or put less effort into your workouts as their will be a little friendly competition to each session.
